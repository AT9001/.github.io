Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a course?

You can join a course by filling out the admission form on our website or by contacting us directly. Once we receive your application, we'll guide you through the enrollment process and payment options.

What equipment do I need for online classes?

You'll need a computer or smartphone with internet access. For some courses (like drawing), you may need specific materials which we'll inform you about after enrollment. For computer courses, having your own computer is recommended but not mandatory.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 100% refund if you're not satisfied after the first two classes. After that, refunds are prorated based on the number of classes attended. Please refer to our refund policy for more details.

Are certificates provided after course completion?

Yes, we provide certificates upon successful completion of all course requirements. Our certificates are recognized by many employers and educational institutions.

What if I miss a live class?

All live classes are auto recorded and made available to enrolled students. You can access the recording and catch up on what you missed. You can also schedule a one-on-one session with the instructor if you have specific questions.
